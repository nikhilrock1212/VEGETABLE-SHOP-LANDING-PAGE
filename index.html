VARIETIES

Tomatoes

Tomatoes are vibrant, versatile fruits widely used in culinary applications. Known for their juicy, succulent texture and tangy-sweet flavor. Tomatoes are rich in essential nutrients like vitamin C, potassium, and antioxidants.

Onions

Onions are versatile, pungent vegetables widely used in culinary applications around the world. Available in various varieties, they add distinct flavor and aroma to dishes, whether raw in salads or cooked in a myriad of cuisines.

Cucumbers

Cucumbers are crisp, cylindrical vegetables with a mild, refreshing taste. Belonging to the gourd family, they are often enjoyed raw in salads, pickled, or used as a crunchy addition to various dishes.

Carrots

Carrots are root vegetables with a crisp texture and sweet taste. They are rich in beta-carotene, a precursor to vitamin A, promoting eye health and providing antioxidants.

French Beans

French beans, also known as haricots verts, are slender and tender green pods harvested from common bean plants. These beans offer a delicate flavor, making them a popular vegetable in culinary dishes worldwide.

Potatoes

Potatoes are starchy, versatile tuberous vegetables, belonging to the Solanaceae family. Widely consumed worldwide, they come in various varieties and can be prepared in numerous ways, such as boiling, baking, or frying.
